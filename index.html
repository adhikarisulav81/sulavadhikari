Web Developer

As being a Web Developer, I am responsible for designing and construction of websites. I need to ensure that sites meet user's expectations by ensuring they look good, run smoothly and offer easy access points with no loading issues between pages or error messages.

Frontend Developer

As being a Front End Developer, my main goal is to combine technology and design to create inviting, easy-to-use websites for consumers. I need to work closely with other Web Developers and IT professionals to design and troubleshoot unique software and web applications.

Blogger & Content Writer
